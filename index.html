ABOUT ME

My whole life I've been plugged in and fascinated as computers and the internet changed the world. Naturally, I pursued a career in IT and I became a technician for family entertainment centers where I repaired arcade games and administrated SOHO type infrastructure.

I stepped away from IT for a few years to learn more about the world and about myself. I did an Americorps service year, then I worked as a medical assistant and nurse's aide. I had chosen to pursue nursing informatics as a career that would combine my passions for IT and helping others. I was attended nursing school pursuant that goal until the pandemic put a stop to that! Now, I've reoriented and am studying IT and development more generally. I am currently enrolled in a bachelors program for software development, and I am also attending SavvyCoders Bootcamp. I joined Savvy Coders to help me develop the skills that I need to jump into the job market right away.

My Savvy Coders capstone was inspired by my Americorps experience building community gardens to mitigate food insecurity in communities across the country. I am building a platform for gardeners to sell their home-grown produce, and for consumers to purchase fresh food from their neighbors.
